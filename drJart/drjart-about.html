About Dr+Jart

Innovative
skincare from Korea.

Dr.Jart+ is not a doctor. Dr.Jart+ is a philosophy.
It stands for doctor joins art.

Inspiration

Dr.Jart+ was founded in Seoul in 2005 by Leeo Lee. hile working as an architect, Leeo was introduced to a makeup-like blemish balm that was only available in dermatology clinics. Inspired by its hybrid functionality, he began collaborating with labs to create a product that blended powerful skincare benefits with the coverage of a foundation.

We believe a good idea can come from anywhere.

We’re inspired by everything from the latest breakthroughs in aerospace, microbiology and material sciences to art and culture.

Dr.Jart+ products are known for proprietary ingredient complexes developed in our skin-science labs.
